## Summary

Ethereum researchers discovered a high-severity attack vector in the ERC4337 standard, which the Ethereum Foundation rewarded with a $50,000 bug bounty. This highlights the importance of rigorous security testing and proactive vulnerability identification in blockchain and AI systems.
