A supply chain attack compromised the Notepad++ updater for 6 months, highlighting critical security vulnerabilities in software distribution infrastructure. This underscores the importance of robust update mechanisms and verifying the integrity of third-party dependencies.

## Content

Suspected China-state hackers used update infrastructure to deliver backdoored version.
