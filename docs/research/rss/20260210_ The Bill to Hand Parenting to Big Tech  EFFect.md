## Summary

Proposed legislation seeks to delegate internet child protection to tech companies, potentially creating a mechanism for mandatory digital identification and age verification that could serve as a gateway for broader surveillance infrastructure.

## Analysis

These 'child protection' bills often create backdoor systems for mandatory digital ID, age verification technologies that can be leveraged for mass tracking, and give tech companies unprecedented gatekeeping power over internet access - classic trojan horse for surveillance expansion
