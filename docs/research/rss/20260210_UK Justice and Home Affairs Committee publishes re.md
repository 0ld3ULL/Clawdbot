## Analysis

Electronic monitoring represents a direct expansion of state surveillance infrastructure, enabling real-time tracking of individuals through mandatory digital tracking devices or systems. The report indicates potential normalization of continuous monitoring as a governance tool, creating granular control mechanisms that can effectively 'switch off' individual mobility and privacy rights.
