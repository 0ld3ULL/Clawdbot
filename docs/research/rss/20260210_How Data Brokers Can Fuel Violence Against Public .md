## Summary

Data brokers are systematically collecting and selling personal information about public servants, creating vulnerability to potential harassment and violence. This infrastructure allows unauthorized tracking and exposure of government workers' private details without meaningful legal protection.

## Original Content

A new report from the Public Service Alliance finds state privacy laws offer public servants few ways to protect their private data, even as threats against them are on the rise.
